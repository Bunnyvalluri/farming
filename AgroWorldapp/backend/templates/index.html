Project Abstract

It is a farming application that helps farmers to track climate data and forecast information as well as find manufacturers and transporters from which the farmer can purchase seeds at an affordable price. The farmer can also communicate with the owner of the vehicle through the application. Additionally, this app provides information regarding crops, flowers, fruits, diseases, and how to expand skills to farmers.
