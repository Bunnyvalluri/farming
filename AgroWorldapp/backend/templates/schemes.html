Government Schemes (Yojanas)

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi

Financial benefit of Rs 6000/- per year is provided to all landholding farmers' families.

  • Who is eligible: All farmer families
  • Benefit: Rs 6,000 / year in 3 installments
Apply Now

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Crop insurance scheme offering financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage.

  • Coverage: Natural calamities, pests, diseases
  • Premium: Very low for farmers (1.5% - 2%)
Apply Now

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Provides timely credit support from banking system for agriculture and allied activities.

  • Loan limit: Flexible based on crop type
  • Interest rate: Subsidized (often 4%)
View Details

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana

Promotion of commercial organic farming through cluster approach.

  • Govt aid: Rs 50,000 per hectare for 3 years
  • Goal: Improve soil health & organic production
